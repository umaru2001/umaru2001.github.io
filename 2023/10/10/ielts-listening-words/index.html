通过一些句子增加雅思听力词汇（1）

English
发布日期:   2023-10-10
文章字数:   1.7k

No.1 What Galileo proposed was that sunspots were indeed a feature of the sun.

伽利略提出的理论是太阳黑子归根结底是太阳自身的特征。

No.2 But if you wish to pursue it further, I suggest that you talk to my boss.

但是如果你想要在此之外提出其他的要求的话,我建议你跟我的老板聊聊啊。

No.3 And their mating method is just so impersonal.

他们的交配方式比较不专一。

No.4 And it’s truly amazing how well it can blend itself in with its surroundings.

它这种熟练地与四周混同的隐蔽能力的确令人惊叹。

No.5 Or were scientists misunderstanding the significance of the evidence.

还是说科学家误解了证据的意义。

No.6 In order to have meat for the winter, people would preserve it in salt. not a spice.

为了能在冬季吃上肉，人们会把肉制品先用盐腌制，而不是香料。

No.7 I thought our textbook said the Egyptians imported their glass from other countries.

我想我们教科书上说的是埃及人从其他的国家进口它们的玻璃。

No.8 And wherever these particles settled. they formed a pan, a layer that water couldn’t penetrate.

不管什么时候这些颗粒停留下来了，他们便会形成一快面板，这是一层水分无法渗透的面板。

No.9 You insert it into the machine and you are ready to make copies.

你需要把卡放进机器里，然后读卡复印。

No.10 It accused her of not respecting the past.

社会谴责她说她不尊重过去。

No.11 Exposition is whatever background information you have to reveal to the audience.

叙述是为了说明一切观众需要知道的背景知识。

No.12 Without it stopping to feed and spread pollen from flower to flower, these plants would cease to exist.

如果它不停止喂养花粉并将花粉从一朵花传播到另一朵花，这些植物将不复存在。

No.13 And that brittle surface ice moving is sometimes considered a type of glacier movement depending on which source you are consulting.

脆性表面冰的移动有时被认为是冰川移动的一种，具体取决于您所咨询的来源。

No.14 And more recently, botanists have used modern DNA testing to scan plant samples.

最近，植物学家使用现代 DNA 测试来扫描植物样本。

No.15 In Weatley’s show, the musical sections, well, they didn’t necessarily integrate within the story.

在韦特利的节目中，音乐部分不一定与故事融为一体。

No.16 But as mass production machines were refined in the 19th century, the price of pianos dropped to the point that a larger proportion of the population could afford to own them.

但随着 19 世纪大规模生产机器的改进，钢琴的价格下降到更多人有能力拥有它们。

No.17 During previous interglacial periods, when glaciers were retreated, they left behind open landing in their wakes.

在以前的间冰期，当冰川消退时，它们留下了开放的着陆点。

No.18 You see writers try to capture a movements in time.

你会看到作家们试图及时捕捉一个动作。

No.19 And how did they conduct their research?

他们是怎样进行研究的？

No.20 How does the vibration wave transmitted into the water?

振动波到底是怎样在水中进行传播的？

No.21 And eventually you slip back into an Ice Age.

最终你回到了冰冻状态。

No.22 But APS digestion is designed specifically to handle solid waste.

但是 APS 消化系统就是被用于设计处理固体垃圾。

No.23 The octopus can release a cloud of ink if it feels threatened.

墨鱼在感觉收到威胁的时候会释放云朵状的墨水。

No.24 We encourage anyone who’s interested to check it out.

我们鼓励任何（对她）)感兴趣的人去查看。

No.25 This time, she used brain scanning equipment to monitor activity in certain parts of the brain.

这一次，她使用大脑扫描仪去监控大脑特定区域内的活动。

No.26 Wouldn’t you appreciate positive feedback if you were a professor?

如果是你教授，你难道不会喜欢这样积极的反馈吗？

No.27 These two countries were trying to extend their territorial waters.

这两个国家正在努力去扩展他们的领海。

No.28 You need to create and efficiently enforce a formal legal code.

你需要创建并正式实施一项正式的法典。

No.29 They have to assess your level and steer you into the right class, you know. intermediate…

我们需要评估你的水平，并且把你安排到对应的班级去。

No.30 It attacks a plant or a bush.

它会共计某一植物或者灌木丛。

No.31 She can pretend when driving her toy car.

她可能假装开着她的玩具车。

No.32 So let’s concentrate on one sight.

所以让我们把精力集中在一处遗迹上。

No.33 Most portraits convey the artist’s personal vision.

大部分肖像艺术家表达的是他们自己的看法。

No.34 What if the person renews it?

但要是那个人又续借了呢？

No.35 I want to major in international studies.

但是我想学习国际关系研究专业。

No.36 I was wondering how you choose the works you exhibit every month?

我想知道你是如何选择你每个月的展览产品的？

No.37 The trend reversed itself when the price of the oil and gas declined.

这股潮流被逆转了，（因为）石油和天然气的价格回落了。

No.38 But how does it apply to animals?

但是这个（理论）是如何应用到动物身上去的呢？

No.39 The current loss of biodiversity can be traced to human activity.

当前生物多样性的丧失可以追溯到人类活动。

No.40 So, the ice would expand or constrict.

因此，冰会膨胀或收缩。

No.41 Snowshoe hares can stay lean and lightweight.

雪鞋野兔可以保持苗条和轻盈。

No.42 Exotic plant species not native to the area can move in and compete.

非该地区本土的外来植物物种可以迁入并参与（物种）竞争。

No.43 She retired a few months ago.

她在几个月前退休了。

No.44 The taste of the birch tree doesn’t get transferred to the food.

白桦树的味道不会转移到食物中。

No.45 In particular, the type of perspective that Alberti advocated was called “Linear One-point Perspective”.

特别是阿尔贝蒂所提倡的这种透视类型被称为“线性单点透视”。

No.46 So eventually, people tried to improve on Les Paul’s model, well, to modify it. I should say.

『所以最终，人们试图改进莱斯·保罗的模型，嗯，修改它。』我应该说。

No.47 Each storyteller would specify a particular location and time.

每个故事讲述者都会指定特定的地点和时间。

No.48 True happiness should be something that I can obtain on my own.

真正的幸福应该是我自己能够获得的。

No.49 They may assume the characters come from the author’s imagination.

他们可能认为这些人物来自作者的想象。

No.50 It’s hard to predict if a good service recovery will overcome the negative effect of service failure.

很难预测良好的服务恢复是否能够克服服务故障的负面影响。
