通过一些句子增加雅思听力词汇（3）

English 单词
英语学习
发布日期:   2023-10-17
文章字数:   1.5k

No.98 What happened 750 million years ago to upset that balance?

7.5亿年前发生了什么事打破了这种平衡？

No.99 So how does this relate to their behavior?

这又跟它们的行为有什么关系呢？

No.100 He didn’t let the conventional thinking of his day restrain his ideas.

他没有让当时的传统思想禁锢他的想法。

No.101 An organism is able to physically select where to live and breed.

生物就会在生理意义上选择它们生长繁殖的地方。

No.102 Salmon need cold, clear streams to ensure the survival of their eggs.

鮭鱼需要寒冷的清澈的溪流来确保其卵的存活。

No.103 So it’s no surprise that someone attempted to design an objective way to measure distraction.

所以有人试图设计一种客观的方法来测量注意力分散也是不足为奇的。

No.104 What I wanna focus on is the way the town was built.

我想把精力放在城镇是怎么建成的。

No.105 How do they coordinate their movements and know where they’re supposed to be?

它们是如何协调它们的动作，并且如何知道它们应该飞去哪里？

No.106 And these flowers bloom at night when bats are active.

这些花是在王蝙蝠活跃的晚上开放。

No.107 We know sauropods couldn’t chew food.

我们知道蜥脚类动物不能咀嚼食物。

No.108 So we concluded that there must have been water on the planet at one time- billions of years ago.

因此我们得出结论，曾经大概几十亿年前,火星上一定有水的存在王。

No.109 Well, then you must know that access is restricted.

那么你一定知道使用权是受到限制的。

No.110 Fogging often occurs when a cold surface comes into contact with warm moist air.

当一个冷表面接触了湿热空气时，就起雾了。

No.111 I will contact them tomorrow morning to see if you’re in the system.

我会明早联系他们看你在不在系统里。

No.112 The shell has to crack open to allow the seed to soak up water.

果壳要裂开才能让种子吸收水分。

No.113 Demand soon far exceeded supply and their prices skyrocketed.

需求远超供给,他们的价格飞涨。

No.114 The music had been composed only for the upper classes.

这种音乐是为上层社会的人而创作的。

No.115 These asteroids are composed mostly of rocky material and metals.

这些小行星大部分是由岩石和金属组成的。

No.116 The number of sunspots increased and decreased in a pattern.

太阳黑子的数量以一个特定的规律升和降。

No.117 At first film music didn’t necessarily correspond to what was on the screen.

最开始，电影音乐不一定要和屏幕上的画面相符合。

No.118 I would probably try to improve the content of the newspaper.

我可能会尝试改善报纸的内容。

No.119 When a whale dives, its metabolic rate drops.

当鲸鱼潜水时，它的新陈代谢率会下降。

No.120 Saturn has at least 49 known moons which vary tremendously in size and shape.

土星至少有 49 颗已知的卫星，它们的大小和形状差异巨大。

No.121 And I’d love to have a place to practice my saxophone without worrying about disturbing people.

我很想有一个地方练习萨克斯管而不用担心打扰别人。

No.122 When an asteroid slammed into Earth, it slammed into Earth’s crust.

当小行星撞击地球时，它也撞击了地壳。

No.123 I endured accounting and economics in high school and barely stayed awake.

我在高中时就一直忍受着会计和经济学（的折磨），几乎没有保持清醒。

No.124 Did you register already for your classes next semester?

你已经注册了下一学期的课程了吗？

No.125 Glaciers reflect light and heat.

冰川反射光和热。

No.126 The owners of these grand buildings wanted to decorate the walls to reflect their own affluence and prestige.

这些宏伟建筑的主人想要装饰墙壁以反映他们自己的富裕和声望。

No.127 No one else can see or hear them to verify the accuracy of his report.

没有其他人可以看到或听到他们来验证他的报告的准确性。

No.128 The Sun basically provides Earth with virtually unlimited resources of energy every day.

太阳每天基本上为地球提供几乎无限的能源资源。

No.129 Now, the concept, which originated in Europe was adopted by American city planners.

现在，这个起源于欧洲的概念被美国城市规划者所采用。

No.130 The oil will float on top of the water because of the oil’s lower density.

由于油的密度较低，因此它会浮在水面上。

No.131 Instrument vibrates and sends those vibrations through the air to our eardrums.

乐器振动并将这些振动通过空气发送到我们的耳膜。

No.132 The brain’s ability to accept or ignore visual information.

大脑接受或忽略视觉信息的能力。

No.133 Bacteria and viruses can infect plants.

细菌和病毒可以感染植物。

No.134 And Subsequent Archaeological digs have confirmed this estimate.

随后的考古发掘也证实了这一估计。

No.135 Immense buildings were being erected as symbols of wealth and rights.

巨大的建筑物被拔地而起，作为财富和权利的象征。

No.136 When a bat approaches, the moth can detect the bat’s presence.

当蝙蝠靠近时，飞蛾可以检测到蝙蝠的存在。

No.137 So you’ve got two different approaches to expanding a business.

因此，您有两种不同的方法来扩展业务。

No.138 Are you charging admission?

你们收入场费吗？

No.139 Experiment disputes that theory.

实验反驳了那个理论。

No.140 This particular exhibit departs from the standard ways.

这个展览背离了标准方式。

No.141 The size car he reserved isn’t available.

他预定的车型没有了。

No.142 They calculated the average density and found no difference.

他们计算了平均密度发现没区别。

No.143 The coach should insist that players be on time and insist that they practice hard, and give their best efforts.

教练必须坚持要求运动员按时到，刻苦练习，并尽最大努力。

No.144 How can a society flourish if the workers and soldiers don’t control their desires and emotions?

如果工人和军人不控制自己的欲望和情感，社会如何繁荣发展？
